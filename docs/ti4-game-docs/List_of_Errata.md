This page includes official corrections to the wordings of cards/abilities in the game that have been published in the TI4 [https://images-cdn.fantasyflightgames.com/filer_public/da/df/dadf9f07-78f3-43ac-9cce-dd6b55b24ec2/ti4_living_rules_reference_v1_3_web.pdf Living Rules Reference]

== List of Erratas ==

===[[Action Cards|Bribery]]===
The text of the action card “Bribery” should read as follows: “After the speaker votes on an agenda: Spend any number of trade goods. For each trade good spent, cast 1 additional vote for <u>the outcome on which you voted.</u>”

===[[The Yin Brotherhood#Racial Abilities|Devotion]]===
The text of the Yin Brotherhood’s “Devotion” faction ability should read as follows: “After each space battle round, you may destroy 1 of your cruisers or destroyers '''<u>in the active system</u>''' to produce 1 hit and assign it to 1 of your opponent’s ships '''<u>in that system.</u>'''”

===[[Diplomacy (Strategy card)|Diplomacy]]===
The text of the “Diplomacy” strategy card’s secondary ability should read as follows: “Spend 1 token from your strategy pool to ready up to 2 exhausted planets '''<u>you control.</u>'''”

===[[Action Cards|Direct Hit]]===
The text of the “Direct Hit” action card should read as follows: “After another player’s ship uses ‘Sustain Damage’ to cancel a hit produced by your units <u>or abilities</u>: Destroy that ship.”

===[[The L1Z1X Mindnet#Faction Abilities|Harrow]]===
The text of the L1Z1X’s “Harrow” faction ability should read as follows: “<u>At the end of each round of ground combat,</u> your ships in the active system may use their bombardment abilities against your opponent’s ground forces on the planet.”

===[[The Winnu#Faction Technologies|Hegemonic Trade Policy]]===
The text of the Winnu faction technology “Hegemonic Trade Policy” should read as follows: “Exhaust this card when 1 or more of your units use ‘Production’; swap the resource and influence values of 1 planet you control <u>during that use of ‘Production.’</u>”

===[[Biotic Technologies#Hyper Metabolism|Hyper Metabolism]]===
The text of the “Hyper Metabolism” technology should read as follows: “During the status phase, '''<u>gain 3 command tokens instead of 2.</u>'''”

===[[The Naalu Collective#Flagship|Matriarch]]===
The text of the Naalu flagship “Matriarch” should read as follows: “During an invasion in this system, you may commit fighters to planets as if they were ground forces. '''<u>When combat ends</u>''', return those units to the space area.”

===[[The Xxcha Kingdom#Faction Promissory Note|Political Favor]]===
The text of the Xxcha promissory note “Political Favor” should read as follows: “<u>When</u> an agenda is revealed: Remove 1 token from the Xxcha player’s strategy pool and return it to his reinforcements. Then, discard the revealed agenda and reveal 1 agenda from the top of the deck. Players vote on this agenda instead. Then, return this card to the Xxcha player.

===[[Action Cards|Unstable Planet]]===
The text of the “Unstable Planet” [[Action Cards|action card]] should read as follows: “Action: Choose 1 hazardous planet. Exhaust that planet and destroy '''<u>up to</u>''' 3 infantry on it.”

===[[Action Cards|Veto]]===
The text of the “Veto” action card should read as follows: “<u>'''When'''</u> an agenda is revealed: Discard that agenda and reveal 1 agenda from the top of the deck. Players vote on this agenda instead.”
